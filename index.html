Hi, I am currently an associate research professor with the School of Cyber Science and Technology at Shandong University (SDU) Qingdao Campus. Prior to joining SDU, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the Singapore University of Technology & Design (SUTD). I worked with Dr. Paweł Szałachowski and Prof. Jianying Zhou on post-quantum distributed computing, e.g., blockchain consensus protocol. Prior to joining SUTD, I was a postdoctoral research associate at Lancaster University from Jan. 2018 worked with Dr. Bingsheng Zhang and Dr. Jan Willemson on cryptographic e-voting and zero-knowledge proof, etc. My postdoctoral works with my advisors are one part of Cybernetica and IOHK.

Before that, I passed my oral defense in Dec. 2017 and received my doctorate from Harbin Engineering University (HEU) in Mar. 2018, under the supervision of Prof. Chunguang Ma. During my doctoral program, I have been very fortunate to have worked with Prof. Steven Galbraith and Prof. Hong-Sheng Zhou, respectively, as a research assistant (and a joint Ph.D. program student) from Dec. 2015 - Oct. 2017. Further, I received the 2019 HEU Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award.

Research Interests: My primary research interests are in cryptographic protocols, secure distributed computing, and privacy-enhancing technologies, including lattice-based cryptography, homomorphic encryption, multiparty computation, and blockchain consensus. Recently, specific topics include: compute and query on encrypted data, verifiable computation in outsourced environments, and password-based cryptography.

I am leaving the job market and joining Shandong University Qingdao campus as a faculty member. Qingdao is the place in which I was born.
